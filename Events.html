Event Type

Accession Lot Number numbers are assigned to all activities related to objects, such as the purchase, donation, exhibition, or deaccession of an object or group of objects. The number represents the activity itself. For group activities, each object in the acquisition, exhibition, or deaccession is assigned the same Accession Lot Number in order to eliminate repetitive data entry. Numbering schemes vary by type of activity.

  • Exhibition - Outgoing Loan: Amon Carter was not a venue
  • Exhibition - ACMAA Organized: Exhibition the Carter organized
  • Exhibition - Outside Organized: Exhibition where we were a venue, but it was not organized by the Carter
  • Exhibition - ACMAA Collection: Organized by the Carter, a formalized "exhibition" of permanent collection items, different from a rotation. There is a formal title, a longer date, etc.
  • Opening- A special event surrounding an exhibition, will mainly be event photography
  • Rotation- ACMAA Collection: Organized by the Carter and a rotation of permanent collection items, different from a formalized "exhibition" of permanent collection items

Event Locality

Indicates whether the exhibition was internally or externally created.

  • Internal- The Amon Carter Museum is a venue for the event.
  • External- A work of the Amon Carter's is a part of an exhibition, but that exhibition is not touring here.
  • Unknown- A historical exhibition for an object. We are unsure of the origin and venues of said exhibition.
